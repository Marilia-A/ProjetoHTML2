Biblioteca de Jogos

GRIS is an emotional journey through a world made of colors. Explore surreal landscapes and restore vibrancy with beautiful music and art. Perfect for students who love artistic adventures!

NEVA is an upcoming adventure game where you bond with a wolf cub in a dying world. Focus on exploration, emotion, and stunning hand-drawn visuals. Great for emotional storytelling fans.

Stardew Valley is a relaxing farming sim. Build your farm, befriend villagers, mine, fish, and uncover secrets in a charming pixel-art world. Ideal for chill gaming sessions.
